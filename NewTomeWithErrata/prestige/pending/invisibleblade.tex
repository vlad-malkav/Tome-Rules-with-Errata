\smallskip\noindent All of the following are Class Features of the Invisible Blade class.

\ability{Weapon and Armor Proficiency:}{The invisible Blade does gain proficiency with any new weapons or armor.}

\ability{Invisible Blade (Sp):}{As a swift action, the Invisible Blade may turn any weapons she wields invisible. Any any target attacked by such a weapon is considered flat-footed unless the target is able to see it (though detect invisible, blindsight, or a similar ability).}

\ability{See Invisible (Su):}{The Invisible Blade can see invisible, as the spell, except that it is always active.}

\ability{Phase Blade (Sp):}{The Invisible Blade can use \spell{wraithstrike} a number of times per day equal to her class level plus 1/2 her character level.}

\ability{Imaginary Blade (Su):}{As a swift action, the Invisible Blade create an invisible weapons of force. These take the form of any light, bladed, non-reach weapon that the Invisible Blade is proficient in, save that they are a force effects and deal force damage. An Imaginary Blade gains an enhancement bonus as a magical weapon. An Imaginary Blade is invisible as the Invisible Blade's eponymous ability.}
